### Example Content - Burleigh Heads

```
Burleigh Heads has a unique character - those leafy streets, the mix of 
original beach shacks and modern architecture, the way the afternoon light 
hits the hillside homes. We've been renovating in Burleigh since 2005, and 
we understand what makes these homes special.

The challenge with Burleigh renovations? Many of these homes were built 
in the 70s and 80s with layouts that don't match how we live now. Closed-off 
kitchens. Small bathrooms. No indoor-outdoor flow. But the location is 
irreplaceable - so you renovate instead of moving.

We recently completed a whole-home renovation on Fourth Avenue (you can 
see it in our projects). The owners wanted to keep that relaxed Burleigh 
vibe while opening up the living spaces. We removed walls, added bifolds 
out to the deck, and used natural materials that fit the coastal setting.

Common Burleigh renovation requests:
- Opening up 70s/80s brick homes
- Coastal kitchen renovations
- Adding decks/outdoor kitchens
- Updating bathrooms with natural stone
- Full character home renovations
```

## üìù Content Guidelines

### Always Include:
- Location mentions (natural, not forced)
- Real project references
- Specific details (shows authenticity)
- Clear CTAs
- Internal links to related pages

### Never Do:
- Generic filler content
- Copy-paste descriptions
- Keyword stuffing
- Make promises you can't keep
- Forget mobile formatting

### Remember:
- You're talking to homeowners, not builders
- They're nervous about renovations (big investment)
- They want to trust you before they call
- Show expertise through specifics, not claims
